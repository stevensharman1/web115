Brand

Welcome to Speedy Snail Branding!

This page showcases my personal brand for WEB115 projects. The Speedy Snail represents a combination of speed in learning and attention to detail.

  • Logo Concept: A snail moving quickly to symbolize consistent progress.
  • Color Palette: Soft browns and greens, warm and approachable.
  • Typography: Clean, modern sans-serif fonts for readability.
  • Personal Brand Statement: "Slow and steady in quality, fast in learning."

Future additions may include brand animations, Favicon icons, and branding guidelines for my web projects.
